Privacy Policy

At Tech Craft Limited, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information. Since we are not yet a registered data collection center, we do not directly collect, store, or process user data on our servers.

1. How We Handle User Data

All data entered through our website forms is securely collected and managed by Typeform, a third-party platform that complies with global data protection standards. Typeform stores the data safely on its servers . we never download or keep a copy of your submissions.

2. Automated Feedback & Emails

When you submit a form, Typeform transmits your data to our automation tool in an encrypted format. Our automation system uses only specific encrypted placeholders (for example, anonymous IDs) to identify your submission and trigger immediate responses such as confirmation emails or feedback messages.

3. No Direct Access to User Data

Our team can only view but not, store, or link your responses to personal information. All automation is controlled through secure APIs and encryption keys to maintain confidentiality at every step.

4. User Control

If you wish to modify or delete your information, you can reach out directly via Typeform’s privacy tools or contact us through our email below. We’ll ensure your request is securely processed through the proper data handler.

5. Transparency & Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as our operations grow and as we become an authorized data controller. Any updates will be reflected on this page with the latest date of revision.
